Deep Dive: Socioeconomic Status

Philly Streets

Live Zoom 4:30 PM - 7:15 PM

Watch the documentary below on Kensington, Philadelphia. It explores the intersection of deindustrialization, addiction, and gentrification.

Philly Streets: Kensington Avenue

Channel 5 News

Self-Reflective Questions

  • Systemic Forces: The documentary links Kensington's crisis to historical events like deindustrialization. How does this history challenge the narrative that poverty is just a lack of "hard work"?
  • Gentrification: The video contrasts the "new" development with the existing streets. Who is "safety" really for in this context, and who gets displaced?
  • The Ethics of Viewing: The narrator questions the "content economy" of filming suffering. Did you feel like a witness or a voyeur while watching? How does your own class privilege shape your reaction?
  • Economic Survival: We see dealers discussing the economics of the trade. How does the lack of legal economic opportunity drive the underground economy in neglected neighborhoods?

Use these questions to guide your Discussion Board #1 post (Due Feb 15).
