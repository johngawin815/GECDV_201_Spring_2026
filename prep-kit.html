Deconstructing Privilege

"Privilege" is a contested term. Watch these four distinct perspectives. Your goal is not to agree with all of them, but to understand how the definitions and arguments differ.

Breakout Discussion Prep

  • Amaechi describes privilege as "the absence of inconvenience." How does this definition compare to Shapiro’s view of privilege as "individual decision making"?
  • Jay Shetty uses a race metaphor. Is this a helpful simplification, or does it miss the complexity that Peterson or Shapiro emphasize?
  • Identify one point of convergence (agreement) and one point of fundamental divergence between these four speakers.
