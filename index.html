Do I need a textbook?
No. All readings and videos are provided in Canvas.
What is the camera policy?
Cameras are expected to support community and participation (30% of grade). Background blur is welcome. If you have bandwidth issues, message the instructor.
How is attendance taken?
We will not be using exit tickets this semester. Attendance will be taken via Zoom analytic tools. Full attendance points will be rewarded each week for coming to class and participating with your zoom camera on.
Is ChatGPT allowed?

Yes, for brainstorming and editing, but not for creating content. You must document its use. Example citation:

OpenAI. (2026). ChatGPT (Feb 2026 version) [Large language model]. https://chat.openai.com/

Your work must reflect your authentic voice.
