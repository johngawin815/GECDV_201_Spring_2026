Do I need to buy a textbook?

No. You do not need to purchase any books. All required readings, videos, and podcasts are provided directly via our Course Hub.

Do I need a working webcam?

Yes. Cameras are expected to be on during Zoom sessions to support community building and active participation (which counts for 30% of your grade).

We understand life happens‚Äîbackground blur is welcome, and if you have temporary bandwidth issues, please message the instructor so we can accommodate you.

Is Generative AI allowed?

Yes, with clear boundaries. You may use generative AI tools (such as ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude) for brainstorming, outlining, proofreading, or polishing your work. However, you may not use them to generate the core content of your essays.

If you use AI assistance, you must document it. Example citation:

OpenAI. (2026). ChatGPT (Feb 2026 version) [Large language model]. https://chat.openai.com/

Bottom line: Use these tools to support your thinking, not replace it. Your work must always reflect your own authentic voice and lived experience.
